Slideshows

Choose Slideshow... from the Album menu to view the current photo album as a full-screen slideshow.

You can set the slideshow options by selecting Slideshow from the Options menu.

Images are displayed once every N seconds and are displayed in the same order as the images in the album. When the slideshow reaches the last image in the album it will advance to the first image in the album if the Repeat Slides button is checked.

Click the left mouse button or press the Spacebar key to quickly advance to the next image in the photo album. Click the right mouse button or press the Backspace key to quickly rewind to the previous image in the photo album. Press the Escape key to stop the slideshow and return to the flPhoto window.
