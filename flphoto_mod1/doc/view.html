The View Menu

The View menu controls how the current image is displayed in flPhoto:

  • Auto Zoom
  • 25%
  • 50%
  • 100%

Choose Auto Zoom to automatically scale the image to fit inside the flPhoto window. Choose 25%, 50%, or 100% to scale the image to a percentage of its actual size.
